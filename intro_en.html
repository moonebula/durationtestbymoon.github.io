Experiment Introduction

Thank you for participating!

This experiment aims to study time perception in digital interfaces. We appreciate your time and contribution.

No name, IP, location, or personally identifiable information will be collected. Only the time (in milliseconds) when you skip the animation will be recorded for anonymous analysis.

On the next screen, you will see a loading animation. Please stay focused, and when you feel it is taking too long and wish to skip, simply click anywhere on the screen.

The experiment will automatically end within 15 seconds, and the data will be submitted automatically.
No need to return to this page or fill in anything else.

Take a deep breath and relax before starting. When you're ready, please fill in the information below and begin.
